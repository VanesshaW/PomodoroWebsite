Welcome to the DORO support page. Here you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and helpful resources to make the most of your DORO experience.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.
How do I start using DORO?
Simply sign up for an account, set your tasks, and start your first Pomodoro session. DORO will guide you through the process.
Can I customize the Pomodoro intervals?
Yes, DORO allows you to customize both your work intervals and break times to suit your personal preferences and work style.
